ABOUT

Amelia Kim

I am a contemporary interdisciplinary fine artist exploring the boundaries between traditional and digital mediums. My practice investigates themes of identity, temporality, and spatial relationships.

Philosophy

Art is a powerful tool for communication and transformation. Through deliberately crafted works, I invite contemplation and personal interpretation, challenging viewers to engage with subjective realities.
